                                Best Practices ​

   Nix is a powerful and flexible tool that offers various approaches to
   accomplish tasks, which can sometimes make it challenging to determine the
   most suitable method for a particular job. To assist you in navigating
   through this vast ecosystem, I have compiled some best practices that I've
   learned from the community. I hope these practices prove helpful to you.
