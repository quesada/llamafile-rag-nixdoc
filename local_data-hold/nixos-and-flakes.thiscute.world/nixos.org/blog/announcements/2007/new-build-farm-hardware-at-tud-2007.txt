                         New build farm hardware at TUD

   Published on Fri Feb 23 2007

                         New build farm hardware at TUD

   New build farmTo quote Eelco Visser: new hardware for buildfarm at Delft
   University of Technology has arrived.

   Here’s what we have: 5 Intel Core 2 Duo DualCore machines with 1GB RAM, 2
   Mac minis with 1,83-GHz Intel Core Duo-processor, another Core 2 Duo a UPS
   to deal with spikes in power supply, a console with integrated monitor and
   keyboard switches, a rack with room for a couple more machines.

   Here’s what we’re going to do with the goodies. The five Intel machines
   and the two MacMinis (also Intel) are going to be used to crank at
   building hundreds of software packages. Using virtualisation we should be
   able to run builds on multiple operating system distributions. Read more…
