     * filterSourceGitignore: Automatic filterSource by parsing .gitignore
       files from within nix

       nix-gitignore tries to convert the fnmatch(3) strings in a .gitignore
       file to perl regexes valid in nix’s match. filterSourceGitignore opts
       for translation to nix boolean predicates instead and implements a
       subset of the gitignore logic as nix functions, aborting for
       unsupported parts.
