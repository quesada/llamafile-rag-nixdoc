                                 New NixOS ISOs

   Published on Wed Feb 06 2008

                                 New NixOS ISOs

   NixOS installer online help New NixOS installation CD images for i686 and
   x86_64 are available, which is a good thing as the previous ones were
   already a few months old. The new images are Nix 0.11-based, contain
   Memtest86+ as a convenience, should support more SATA drives, and show
   online help (the NixOS manual) on virtual console 7.
