                    Stdenv updates branch merged into master

   The stdenv-updates branch has been merged into the master branch of
   Nixpkgs. The main change are that brings is that Nixpkgs/NixOS are now
   based on GCC 4.8 and Glibc 2.18, in addition to many smaller updates.
