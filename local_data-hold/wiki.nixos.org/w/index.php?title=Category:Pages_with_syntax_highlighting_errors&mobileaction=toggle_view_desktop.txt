   From NixOS Wiki

   SyntaxHighlight adds pages to this tracking category if they have a
   missing or unsupported lang="…" attribute in a <source or
   <syntaxhighlight> tag.

   Common problems and solutions:

    1. a missing attribute
         1. Look for a specific Supported language
         2. Add lang="text"
    2. the unsupported attribute lang="sh"
         1. Add lang="bash"
         2. Add lang="shell-session" or lang="console"
    3. the unsupported attribute lang="mediawiki"
         1. Add lang="wikitext"
