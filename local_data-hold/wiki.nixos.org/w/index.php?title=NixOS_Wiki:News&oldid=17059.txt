   What's new with the wiki?

News

    2024-09-02 — Wiki editors use Manual of Style

   The Wiki team discussed and put into effect the Manual of Style for
   editing wiki articles. This is a huge step towards clear and consistent
   documentation. All editors are encouraged to follow this guide.

    2024-08-22 — Wiki editors meeting

   All interested wiki editors meet on 22th of August at 21:15 CEST in a
   Jitsi conference.

   Agenda: https://pad.lassul.us/wiki-agenda?view

    2024-07-05 — Wiki editors meeting

   All interested wiki editors meet on 5th of July at 13:00 CEST in a Jitsi
   conference.

    2024-04-01 — Launch of the wiki

   The official NixOS wiki has taken off! If you want to find out why there
   is a new wiki effort, check out the FAQ.

    2017-08-21 — More content
