   From NixOS Wiki

   All files and folders in /nix/store match the regular expression

 [0-9a-z]{32}-[-.+_?=0-9a-zA-Z]+

     * [0-9a-f]{32} is the hash
     * [-.+_?=0-9a-zA-Z]+ is the name
