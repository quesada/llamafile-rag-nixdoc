   From NixOS Wiki

   This article extends nixos.org/community for how to get in touch and lists
   additional platforms to meet community members.

   See the Nix Community article to learn more about who we are.

Official Spaces

   These are spaces are moderated by the NixOS Moderation Team

  Events

   See the Events page for past and upcoming events!

   See also the NixCon Fediverse or YouTube sites for past NixCon recordings.

  Github

     * NixOS - Official Nix Org
          * Contribute packages on nixpkgs
          * Develop the nix language
          * Participate in RFC discussions
     * Nix-Community - Explore community projects!

  Forums

     * Official Discourse forum - Ask for help here!

  Chats

    Matrix

   The official NixOS Community Space has rooms on many Nix topics.

     * #users:nixos.org - Main Room
     * #wiki:nixos.org - Wiki Contributors

   Note: If your client doesn't support Matrix Spaces, see the MatrixRooms
   page for list of all rooms.

    Zulip

     * NixOS Governance Discussion - Ongoing NixOS Governance discussions
       occurring here. Requires contribution to nixpkgs to participate.

    Jitsi

     * OfficeHours - Weekly Nix Office Hours (1600-2000 UTC Fridays, more
       times as mentors are available)

Officially Maintained

   Unmoderated spaces maintained by the moderation team.

    Mastodon

    Twitter

    LinkedIn

Unofficial Spaces

   Spaces maintained by members of the community.

  Forums

     * r/NixOS on Reddit (>25k members)
     * !nix@programming.dev on Lemmy (>1k subscribers)
     * Stack Overflow (>1k questions answered)
     * Unix & Linux Stack Exchange (>400 questions answered)

  Chats

    Discord

     * Nix/NixOS (Unoffical)
     * Nix/NixOS Community^[1]

    IRC

   Previously hosted on Freenode but was switched to libera.chat. There are
   public logs from the Freenode versions of these channels.

     * #nixos - General NixOS and Nix help
     * #nix-darwin - Nix on macOS
     * #nixos-chat - Informal chit-chat channel when going off-topic in the
       main channel
     * #nixos-dev - Nix and NixOS development (#nixos for questions)
     * #nixos-emacs - Nix Emacs discussion

      Regional IRC

     * #nixos-cs - Czech and Slovak NixOS community
     * #nixos-fr - French NixOS community
     * #nixos - German NixOS community on hackint.org (webchat)

    Regional Telegram

   There are some regional Telegram groups dedicated to NixOS:

     * @NixOS_zhcn - Chinese, English
     * @ru_nixos - Russian, English
     * @nixos_en - English
     * @nixosbr - Portuguese
     * @nixosita - Italian
