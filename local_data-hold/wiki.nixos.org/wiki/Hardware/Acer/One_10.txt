   Acer One 10  
   A Pinebook 11".
   Manufacturer Acer        
   Architecture x86_64      
   Bootloader   32-bit GRUB 
   Maintainer   KREYREN     

   Work in progress page on documenting NixOS compatibility with the device
   with additional useful informations

Status

   The device boots NixOS, but sometimes freezes and the WiFi doesn't work

Known issues

  Booting

   The device is capable of running a 64-bit operating system, but it's
   bootloader can only be 32-bit...
