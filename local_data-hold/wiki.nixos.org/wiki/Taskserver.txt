   From NixOS Wiki

   Taskserver is the server component of version 2 of Taskwarrior, a free and
   open source todo list application. Starting from version 3 of Taskwarrior
   sync is not done via Taskserver anymore: see [1].

   Taskserver is documented in the NixOS manual.
